Tables:
    Contents of a table is ept inside <table></table> tags\
    <tr>
        <td>Cell value: a[i][j]= a[1][1]
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Cell value: a[i][j]= a[2][1]
    </tr>

    tr = container for values of a row in table
    td = date for a cell in its specific row
    th= table header
    <td>&nbsp;</td> = non-breaking space (&nbsp;)
    <caption> places a caption directly beneath the <table> tag.
